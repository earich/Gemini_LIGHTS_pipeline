List of all files used in reduction for this object, FU Ori.

For N raw data files, should end up with:
N   x podc
N   x PSFchecked_podc, where bad centres are overwritten
N/4 x podc_stokesdc, interim stokes datacubes
1   x combined_stokesdc, mean of the interim stokes datacubes
1   x combined_rstokesdc, [I,Qphi,Uphi,V] of the above
1   x maps.png, quick look image of total I, LPI, Qphi, Uphi

n.b. The sanitycheck, PSFcheck, and recipe files tend to be saved elsewhere.
I move them all into one place when the reduction is complete.
